Hair We Share

Value Proposition: Combating the effects of medical hair loss.

Vision: To provide quality solutions to medical hair loss for anyone in the USA that needs them, free of charge. Hair We Share aims to restore the confidence of those that have lost hair due to medical treatments.

Visit Their Website »

How Do They Do It?

Hair Donations

Hair We Share accepts hair that is:

  • At least 12 inches long
  • Without bleach or highlights
  • Hair that is a natural color (Ex: Blonde, Brown, Grey)

Donate Hair »

Monetary Donations

Alongside hair donations, Hair We Share uses donations to fund the creation of their wigs. This allows them to provide their wigs free of charge to those who need them. They ask for a minimum donation of $25 with each ponytail, but all donations are welcome.

Make a Donation »

Tracking Program

If you donate $125 with your hair, you will get updates about your hair. You can see your hair in a wig within 6 months. Rarely, you may even get to see the recipient of your wig!

Adopt a Ponytail »


How Do You Get Involved?

As mentioned above, the best ways are to make monetary and/or hair donations. To prepare for these donations you can:

Grow Your Hair →

As mentioned earlier, you should grow your hair out to at least 12 inches before making a donation. Also, please clean and dry your hair before you send it to Hair We Share.

Make A Donation →

If you are ready to cut your hair, please be sure to include a $25 donation minimum with it to ensure that Hair We Share can continue to produce quality wigs for those in need.

Track Your Ponytail

Please consider making a donation of $125 to be able to track your hair and see the wig it becomes a part of.
