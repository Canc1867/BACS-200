Weight Lifting

The key to a great looking physique can come from a variety of different workouts.

After years of research and trial and error, people have discovered the essential ways to create the body of their dreams.

  • Weight Lifting - Resistent training through light or heavy weights.
  • Body Weight Lifts - Creating tension on your muscles through body weight workouts.
  • Motivation - Having the desire and will power to push yourself past your limits.
  • Commitment - Being consistent with your daily routines to ensure success.

Week 1

Monday Bench Press: 3 x 10, Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 x 10, Cable Fly’s 3 x 10, Tricep Pull Down (w/ rope) 3 x 10, Skull Crusher 3 x 10, Dips 3 sets (till failure each set), Abs of Choice

Wednesday Squats 3 x 10, Calf Raise’s 3 x10, Leg Extensions 3 x 10, Hamstring curls 3 x 10, Dumbell Lunge’s 3 x 20

Friday Lat Pulldown 3 x 10, Seated Row 3 x 10, Bicep Curls (with straight bar) 3 x 10, Bent over Dumbbell Rows 3 x 10 (each arm), Hammer curls 3 x 10, Seated Incline bicep curls (w/ dumbbell) 3 x 10 (each arm), Abs of Choice

Week 3 -Change rep scheme to 4 x 8 (Use for all lifts)

Week 6 (Strength Phase) -Change rep scheme to 4 x 5 of Core lifts (Bench, Squat, Lat Pull Down, Seat Row, Bicep Curl (Straight Bar) -Everything else continue 4 x 8

Week 8 -Change rep scheme to 8, 6, 4, 2 of Core lifts (Pyramid Method) -Everything else use 3 x 8-12

P.S. You can always substitute or add lifts after a few weeks to change it up a little. (Make sure protein intake is reasonably high. 1g per lb of body weight, do your best)
